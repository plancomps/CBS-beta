Funcons-beta declaration not found

A URL of the form .../CBS-beta/Funcons-beta#id should redirect to a web page that displays the declaration of the funcon indicated by id. For example, …CBS-beta/Funcons-beta#Name:scope redirects to …CBS-beta/math/Funcons-beta/Computations/Normal/Binding/#Name:scope.

Redirection to the present page indicates that the funcon indicated by id is not declared in the Funcons-beta collection. This could be due to the omission of the Name: prefix, or a spelling error.
